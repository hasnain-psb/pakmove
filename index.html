About Our Mission

Pioneering the Future of Pakistan's Express Logistics

Founded in 2026, PakMove was built to resolve traditional transit delays, billing transparency gaps, and courier insecurity within Pakistan's rapid delivery sector.

By pairing high-tech routing protocols with flexible vehicle fleets, we serve local businesses, e-commerce stores, and individuals across Karachi, Lahore, Rawalpindi, Peshawar, and Islamabad.
